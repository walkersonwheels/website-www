    About Us

    Who we are

    We are a motivated group of individuals who are focused on improving the world by any means. We aim to achieve equity globally and we strongly believe the best way to achieve that is through providing the same opportunities. To give a unified starting point, we need to ensure that children have access to education, resources and in the future, appropriate employment opportunities. On foot, a human will take statistically 3 hours to complete a 10 kilometer journey. While on one of our scooters, 20 minutes. How can we say no when we can lend a "foot" to those who need it more?

    Spending more time in school, commuting further for a brighter business opportunity, what city-dwellers enjoy are critical to the betterment of our beneficiaries' lives.
